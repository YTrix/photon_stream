Photon-Stream (phs)

Observations (obs)

All observed air-shower events of FACT. About 1e9 air-shower records containing about 3e12 single photons. See ./obs/runstatus.csv to get a quick overview of the photon-stream integrity.

Simulations (sim)

The FACT responses to simulated air-shower observations. This is the Dortmund ceres_12.rc simulation pass.
